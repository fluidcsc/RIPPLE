Be part of the Ripple

Tier 1 - The First Drop

This is a Free Tier that gives you early access to board game sign‑ups, invitation to free community events, our monthly community newsletter, a digital welcome pack, and an exclusive The First Drop Member Patch.

Tier 2 - The Crest

For £8/month you get everything in Tier 1, plus 1 free drink per month, 10% off workshops, access to “Maker Mondays” (members‑only craft nights), members‑only seasonal drink tasting, priority booking for events, access to the Ripple Discord (community chat, craft swaps, inclusive space), and an exclusive The Crest Member Patch.

Tier 3 - The Ripple Maker

For £15/month you get everything in Tier 2, plus 3 free drinks per month, 15% off events, access to “After Hours at Ripple” & free entry to monthly “Ripple Society Club Social”, and an exclusive The Ripple Maker Member Patch.
