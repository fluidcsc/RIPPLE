About Us

Brewing connections, one Ripple at a time.

Ripple began with a simple intention: to create a calm, welcoming space where people could slow down, connect, and enjoy coffee crafted with passion and care. Coffee tastes better when people feel welcome, seen, and free to play. Settle in, breathe out, and let the day soften around you.

Inspired by the gentle movement of water & sand, RIPPLE is designed to feel soft, warm, and grounding — a place where every detail encourages you to breathe a little deeper, to make small ripples of interaction that would eventually grow into a positive impact for our shared community. From workshops to cosy socials, everything here is designed to help you feel part of something — gently, naturally, and at your own pace.

Our vision is like having a place to gather like a living room where folks know each other and feel welcome in the space. We'd like to create an inclusive community hub for creatives, friends, families, remote workers, anyone and everyone in-between. We are LGBTQIA+, Neurodivergent, and Accessible friendly. We'd like for you to Begin Mindful, Stay Playful, and Leave Connected.

Ripple is ran by a sibling duo - Jelly & Jo - who both come from a Speciality Coffee & Hospitality background and have been living in Edinburgh since 2014. They come from a mixed heritage of Dutch & Filipino, but have both fell for Edinburgh's charm and magic and now call it home. Their café is based in Leith, Edinburgh and are sharing the space with 'Our Collective Spaces' who are separately running a co-working & event space as well as a yoga studio in the same location. The café is open 7 days a week from 9am - 6pm and can be booked from private catered events from 7-9pm. Visit us and come be part of the Ripple!
