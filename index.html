About Us

Brewing connections, one Ripple at a time.

Ripple began with a simple intention: to create a calm, welcoming space where people could slow down, connect, and enjoy coffee crafted with passion and care. Coffee tastes better when people feel welcome, seen, and free to play. Settle in, breathe out, and let the day soften around you.

Inspired by the gentle movement of water & sand, RIPPLE is designed to feel soft, warm, and grounding — a place where every detail encourages you to breathe a little deeper, to make small ripples of interaction that grow into a positive impact for our shared community.

Our vision is to create an inclusive community hub for creatives, friends, families, remote workers, anyone and everyone in-between. We are LGBTQIA+, Neurodivergent, and Accessible friendly.

Ripple is run by a sibling duo — Jelly & Jo — both from a Speciality Coffee & Hospitality background. Their café is based in Leith, Edinburgh, sharing space with Our Collective Spaces who run co-working, events, and a yoga studio.
