Our Story

RIPPLE began with a simple intention: to create a calm, welcoming space where people could slow down, connect, and enjoy coffee crafted with care.

Inspired by the gentle movement of water, RIPPLE is designed to feel soft, warm, and grounding — a place where every detail encourages you to breathe a little deeper.

Since opening in Edinburgh, we’ve grown into a community hub for remote workers, creatives, friends, and families. Every cup we serve reflects our values: thoughtful sourcing, artisan preparation, and genuine hospitality.
